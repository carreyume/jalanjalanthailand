Legend Travel Route
The Mahsuri Trail
Langkawi → Phuket
Follow the legendary footsteps of Mahsuri — a 200-year journey of love, injustice, and a curse that shaped a kingdom.

In the early 1800s, Mahsuri — daughter of Phuket Malay parents who migrated to Langkawi — was falsely accused of adultery while her warrior husband was away at war. Despite bleeding white blood as proof of her innocence, she was executed. With her dying breath, she cursed Langkawi with seven generations of misfortune. Her family fled back to Siam (Thailand). In the year 2000, her seventh-generation descendant Wan Aishah was found living in Kampung Kemala, Phuket — and when she visited Mahsuri's tomb, Langkawi finally began to prosper.
